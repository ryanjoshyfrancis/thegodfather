Vito Corleone played by Marlon Brando is the Head/"GodFather" of the Corleone crime family which is operated behind the Genco olive oil company.

The Corleone is also part of the five crime families in New York which contains Corleone, Tattaglia,Cunea,Barzini,Stracci crime families.

Vito Andolini aka Vito Corleone was born in Corleone,Sicily December 7th 1891 and escaped to New York when his whole family was killed by the local Mafia.

Vito is desicribed as an old intelligent and wise man who can easily read others intentions and a smooth subtle talker who is able to convince others without using bullets.
