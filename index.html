The Godfather

The Godfather was released in August 12,1972

Directed by Francis Ford Coppola.

Marlon Brando as Don Vito Corleone, An aging Head of the Corleone Crime family ,one of the five major Italian Mob families in New York

Al Pacino as Michael Corleon A WWII veteran coming back home for his sister Connie's Wedding alongside his girlfriend Kay Adams.

Taila shire as Connie Corleone ,daugter of Vito Corleone and sister of Michael Corleone.

The story of this film is based around Mario Puzo's novel also called The Godfather. The main premise of this story revolves around a 1940's Italian-American Mafia boss handing over his Empire to his reluctant son and then later on takes over the family Business.
