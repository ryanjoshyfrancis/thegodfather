Francis Ford Coppola

Francis Ford Coppola was born at April 7th 1939 in Detroit,Michigan.

to father Cormaine Coppola a Flautist with the Detroit Symphony Orchestra and mother Italia Pennino, A Housewife.

Francis and his family are Second-generation Italians,the grandparents coming from Basilicata,Italy.

Later on the family moved Queens,NewYork. He spended most of his childhood bedridden suffering polio,There he founded a hobby for Homemade puppet theater productions.

Then Francis Devloped his passion towards theater after reading a novel called "A Street Car named Desire"
